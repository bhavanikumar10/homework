Maximum Temperature

The 'Maximum Temperature' chart aims to establish the relationship between the maximum temperatures observed at different cities to their distance from the equator. We observe that the temperature of the cities starts raising below the equator, peaking at 20degrees latitutde and then starts falling down thereafter.
