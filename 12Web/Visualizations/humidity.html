Humidity

The Humidity plot aims to analyse the humidity percentage levels of the cities before, at and after equator's latitude. The Humidity at 100% is highest at the cities closer to the equator with more cities concentrating at peak humidity at/near the equator. The latitutes below the equator have less humidity, with humidity raising closer to the equator, and then consistenly high humidity levels are seen in cities at and further beyond the equator.
